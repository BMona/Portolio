My story

I have always loved computers since I was a child. I credit this to my father who as a child took exposed me to computer by taking me to a cyber cafe that my uncle owned. I would do simple things such as play around with microsoft and make beautiful presentable decorations on microsoft powerpoint. I remember how people were fascinated by how a simple child at 6 years old could do such things and hence began my passion for the technology world.
